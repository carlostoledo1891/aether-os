# IRA Section 45X — FEOC Rules for Critical Minerals

**Authority:** U.S. Internal Revenue Code §45X (Inflation Reduction Act of 2022, §13401)  
**Treasury guidance:** Final Rule, 26 CFR Part 1 (December 2023, updated March 2024)  
**Scope:** Advanced manufacturing production credit for critical minerals; FEOC exclusion rules.  
**Relevance:** Caldeira's 0% FEOC status enables maximum green premium for U.S. buyers.

---

## Section 45X Production Credit

A per-unit tax credit for domestic production of eligible critical minerals:

| Component | Credit |
|-----------|--------|
| Electrode active materials | 10% of production cost |
| Critical minerals (applicable) | 10% of production cost |
| Battery cells | $35/kWh |
| Battery modules | $10/kWh (if cells also credited: $45 total) |

The critical mineral credit applies to minerals **extracted, processed, or recycled in the U.S. or by a U.S. trade partner** — including Brazil.

---

## Foreign Entity of Concern (FEOC) — §30D(d)(7)

### Definition
A "Foreign Entity of Concern" is any entity that is:
1. **Owned, controlled, or directed by** the government of a covered nation (China, Russia, North Korea, Iran)
2. A **25% or more equity holder** is a covered nation government or entity thereof
3. **Incorporated/headquartered** in a covered nation
4. An entity on the DoC Entity List, BIS Denied Persons List, or Treasury SDN List

### Phase-in Schedule — §30D Clean Vehicle Credit

| Year | Battery Component FEOC Exclusion | Critical Mineral FEOC Exclusion |
|------|----------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| 2024 | Vehicles with FEOC battery components ineligible | — |
| 2025 | Same | Vehicles with FEOC-sourced critical minerals ineligible |
| **2025+** | **Full exclusion** | **Full exclusion** |

### Critical Mineral Percentage Thresholds (§30D(e)(1)(B))

Minimum percentage of critical mineral value from U.S./FTA-partner extraction or recycling:

| Tax Year | Required % |
|----------|-----------|
| 2023 | 40% |
| 2024 | 50% |
| 2025 | 60% |
| 2026 | 70% |
| 2027 | 80% |
| 2028+ | 80% |

## Caldeira FEOC Analysis

### Why Caldeira maintains 0% FEOC

- **Country:** Brazil — not a covered nation; U.S. trade partner
- **Operator:** Meteoric Resources (ASX: MEI) — Australian-listed, no covered nation government equity
- **Processing:** CIP plant in Poços de Caldas — no covered nation involvement in processing chain
- **Supply chain:** Direct mine-to-separated-oxide pathway eliminates intermediary FEOC risk
- **Equity structure:** Publicly auditable shareholder register; no 25%+ covered nation holdings
